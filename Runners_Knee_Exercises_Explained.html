Runner's Knee Exercises

Quick, form-focused reference for the moves most commonly used for patellofemoral / "runner's knee" rehab.

  • Do the Core + Hip control moves first, then the Strength moves.
  • Prioritize quality: slow reps, no pain spike, knee tracks over 2nd/3rd toe.
  • If any move causes a sharp/lingering pain increase, scale it (smaller range, slower, fewer reps) or skip it.

    • Core + Hip control (keeps your pelvis/knee from caving while you run)

    Core Dead Bug

    • Setup: On back, hips/knees ~90 degrees, arms up.
    • Do: Slowly reach opposite arm + leg away while keeping low back gently pressed into the floor (ribs down).
    • Feel: Deep abs; not hip flexors.
    • Common fail: Low back arches - shorten the reach.

    Hip Clamshell

    • Setup: Side-lying, knees bent, heels together, hips stacked.
    • Do: Keep feet together and rotate the top knee up (open the clam) without rolling your pelvis back.
    • Feel: Side-butt (glute med/min), not low back.
    • Common fail: Pelvis rolls open - put your top hand on your hip to lock it.

    Hip Side-lying Leg Raise (Hip Abduction)

    • Setup: Side-lying, bottom knee slightly bent, top leg straight.
    • Do: Lift top leg 6-12 inches, pause, lower slow.
    • Feel: Side-butt; a little outer thigh is ok.
    • Common fail: Toes turn up (hits hip flexor/TFL) - rotate toes slightly down/forward.

    Core/Hip Side Plank (or Side Plank Raises)

    • Setup: Elbow under shoulder, body in a straight line.
    • Do: Hold, or do small controlled hip lifts/lowers.
    • Feel: Obliques + side-butt of bottom hip.
    • Common fail: Shoulder shrugs - push the floor away (long neck).

    Strength (builds capacity so your knee stops taking all the load)

    Glutes Glute Bridge

    • Setup: On back, knees bent, feet flat about hip-width.
    • Do: Exhale, squeeze glutes, lift hips until shoulders-to-knees is a straight line. Pause. Lower slow.
    • Feel: Glutes/hamstrings; not low back.
    • Common fail: Over-arching - keep ribs down, stop when neutral.

    Hinge Romanian Deadlift (RDL)

    • Setup: Stand tall, soft knees, weight close to legs.
    • Do: Push hips back (hinge) with neutral spine, shins fairly vertical. Stand by driving hips forward.
    • Feel: Hamstrings + glutes stretch/effort.
    • Common fail: Squatting it - think "butt to wall."

    Single-leg Split Squat

    • Setup: Staggered stance (like train tracks), tall torso.
    • Do: Lower straight down, front knee tracks over toes, push up through mid-foot/heel.
    • Feel: Front quad + glute; slight stretch in back hip.
    • Common fail: Wobbly narrow stance - widen slightly.

    Knee control Step-Down (video fallback)

    • Setup: Stand on a step/box on one leg.
    • Do: Slowly lower until the free heel taps the floor, then rise. Keep pelvis level; knee tracks over toes.
    • Feel: Standing-leg quad + glute, controlled lowering.
    • Common fail: Knee caves inward - reduce step height and slow down.
